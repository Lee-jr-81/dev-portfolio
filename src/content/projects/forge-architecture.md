# Forge Architecture

## Overview

Forge Architecture is a UK-based architectural practice specialising in residential design and small-scale commercial projects.

The studio focuses on delivering thoughtful, well-resolved spaces that balance contemporary design with contextual sensitivity. The objective of the project was to create a digital presence that reflects this precision and craftsmanship while making it easy for prospective clients to explore services and initiate contact.

---

## Challenge

Forge Architecture operates in a competitive regional market where trust, clarity, and credibility are essential.

The key challenges were:

- communicating expertise without overwhelming visitors
- presenting projects in a visually-led, refined format
- positioning the studio as both design-led and technically capable
- simplifying the enquiry process for residential clients unfamiliar with architectural services

The site needed to feel structured, confident, and professional — avoiding anything overly decorative or distracting.

---

## Solution

The website was designed with a minimalist, project-first approach that prioritises imagery and spatial storytelling.

Key implementation areas included:

- **Project-led homepage**  
  Immediate focus on completed work to demonstrate capability and establish credibility

- **Clear service structure**  
  Breakdown of services aligned to RIBA stages, translated into accessible, client-friendly language

- **Case study pages**  
  Dedicated project pages combining imagery, context, and design rationale

- **Refined visual system**  
  Clean typography and a restrained colour palette inspired by architectural drawings and material palettes

- **Streamlined enquiry flow**  
  A simple, low-friction contact process designed to encourage new client enquiries

- **Scalable build**  
  A flexible structure allowing the studio to easily expand its portfolio as new projects are completed

---

## Outcome

The result is a clean, professional digital presence that aligns closely with Forge Architecture’s design philosophy.

The platform provides:

- a scalable foundation for future project additions
- an improved and simplified enquiry process
- a stronger visual identity consistent with the studio’s work

The site positions Forge Architecture as a contemporary, detail-oriented practice, ready to take on residential and small commercial commissions.
