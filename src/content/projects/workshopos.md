# workshopOS

## Overview

workshopOS is a managed platform for specialist vehicle businesses — campervan converters, adventure dealers, and utility vehicle operators who need more than a brochure site.

The public experience covers inventory, conversion packages, upgrades, testimonials, and enquiry capture. A separate admin dashboard handles listings, media, and day-to-day operations without a generic page builder in the way.

The live demo deployment showcases the platform on a campervan conversion business site.

---

## Challenge

Specialist vehicle businesses often outgrow template websites and scattered tools:

- inventory split across portals, social listings, and spreadsheets
- sold builds removed from the site instead of compounding SEO value
- slow, generic CMS workflows that do not match how stock and builds are actually managed
- no calm operational layer for listings, media, and enquiries in one place

The goal was a repeatable platform — premium on the frontend, structured on the backend — that could deploy for different niches within the adventure and utility vehicle space.

---

## Solution

workshopOS was built as a full-stack platform using:

- Next.js (App Router) for SEO-first public pages and a responsive admin experience
- Supabase for authentication, database, and secure multi-tenant-style data access
- Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui for a consistent, accessible UI system

Key implementation areas included:

- **Public vehicle and build pages**  
  Structured listing detail with adventure, utility, and conversion-specific fields — built for search from the ground up

- **Conversion packages and upgrades**  
  Premium presentation of build specs and optional extras as structured content, not ad-hoc blog posts

- **Portfolio and sold-build modes**  
  Support for keeping previous builds live for long-term SEO where that fits the business model

- **Operational dashboard**  
  Listings, galleries, enquiries, and testimonials in a guided admin workflow focused on inventory quality

- **Deployable demo**  
  A production-style deployment for a campervan conversion business, demonstrating the full public and operational stack

---

## Outcome

workshopOS is a production-ready platform foundation for specialist vehicle businesses — combining a fast public site, structured inventory, and a usable admin layer in one deployment.

The demo is live at [workshopos-green.vercel.app](https://workshopos-green.vercel.app/).
