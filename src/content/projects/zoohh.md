# Zoohh

## Overview

Zoohh brings pet records, day-to-day care, and practical owner tools into a single web application.

The product is aimed at pet owners who want a dependable place to log health information, routines, and reminders — without juggling notes, photos, and separate apps.

---

## Challenge

Pet care information is often scattered across:

- ad-hoc notes and messaging threads
- photos and documents stored in different places
- calendars or reminders that are not tied to the animal’s record

The challenge was to keep the experience simple for everyday use while still supporting structured data (dates, categories, and searchable history) that stays useful over time.

---

## Solution

Zoohh was built as a full-stack application using:

- Next.js (App Router) for fast, server-aware pages and a cohesive UX
- Supabase for authentication, database, and secure access to owner data
- Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui for a consistent, accessible interface

Key implementation areas included:

- **Pet profiles and records**  
  A clear model for pets and their history so owners can add and revisit information in one place

- **Care-oriented workflows**  
  Flows designed around recurring care tasks and reminders, aligned with how people actually manage pets day to day

- **Account and data access**  
  Sign-in and row-level patterns so each owner only sees their own data

- **Performance and polish**  
  Responsive layouts and lightweight interactions so the app feels usable on mobile first

---

## Outcome

Zoohh delivers a focused foundation for pet-centred record keeping and owner tooling, with a stack and architecture built to grow as features expand.
